Ecosystem

Decentralized Finance Ecosystem

YDFI - ydfi.finance is a decentralized finance ecosystem (DeFi) that offers, through its platform, several decentralized financial products.

Our platform allows you to borrow assets and earn interest on them. Our staking allows you to receive the Annual Percentage Rate (APR) from 33%. In addition, holders of the YDFI token can vote through the governance token and decide on improvements for the entire ecosystem.

Decentralized

Decentralized Applications for a Decentralized Financial Ecosystem

YDFI.finance offers an ecosystem capable of uniting different financial services in a totally decentralized way. Lend or borrow, Farming, Vote and help improve the ecosystem, among others, are some of the services offered by our decentralized platform.

Through Smart Contracts on Ethereum's public Blockchain, all developments are carried out with the direct participation of the holders of the YDFI token.
